About the event

A comprehensive local event for all levels of professionals involved with technology through the complete lifecycle of a product; from hardware repair to the most complex networking and cyber security practices. Hosted in one central location for participants of all ages, both as hobbyists/novices or experienced systems designers, the Fair allows for the demonstration of technical ability directly to prospective employers across the globe, from some of the most well-known Cloud, AI, and Systems integration organisations in existence today. The Fair will provide you with an opportunity to validate your abilities, solve challenging problems that exist in today’s marketplace, and potentially secure your next major career move at TechNexus.
